# WELCOME TO THE VIBESFLOW

**VibesFlow** is a decentralized music streaming platform that enables real-time collaborative music creation and sharing through blockchain technology. Create solo or group vibestreams, mint them as RTA NFTs, and share your musical frequencies with the world.
